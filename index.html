Learn About Currency Exchange

What is Currency Exchange?

Currency exchange is the process of converting one country's currency into another. This is essential for international trade, travel, and investment.

How Exchange Rates Work

Exchange rates determine how much one currency is worth in another currency. They fluctuate based on economic factors, market demand, and government policies.

Factors Affecting Rates

Interest rates, inflation, political stability, and economic performance all influence currency values in the global market.
